Our Services

Weekly Cleaning Service

Our standard service includes a thorough weekly cleaning of your cat's litter box. We empty, scrub, sanitize, and refill with fresh litter, ensuring your home remains odor-free and your cat has a clean environment.

  • Complete litter replacement
  • Box sanitization with pet-safe products
  • Surrounding area cleaning

Deep Cleaning Service

Our premium deep cleaning service goes beyond the standard cleaning. We perform a comprehensive sanitization of the box and surrounding area, apply deodorizing treatments, and can even replace the entire box if needed.

  • Advanced sanitization techniques
  • Premium litter options available
  • Box replacement service

One-Time Service

Need a one-time deep clean? Perhaps you're preparing for guests or returning from vacation. Our one-time service provides all the benefits of our regular cleaning with no ongoing commitment required.

  • Thorough box cleaning and sanitization
  • Fresh litter replacement
  • No contract needed
